How it works

Simple. Fast. Impactful.

Three easy steps to turn your surplus resources into essentials for someone in need.

01
📦

List Your Donation

Register as a donor and list surplus items — food, clothes, books, or anything useful. Add quantity, location and pickup date.

02
🤖

AI Finds Best Match

Our Gemini AI engine instantly matches your donation with the most suitable verified NGO based on location, category and demand.

03
🤝

NGO Collects & Delivers

The matched NGO claims the donation, coordinates pickup, and delivers to communities. You track status in real time.

Features

Built to make impact

Every feature is designed to make donation seamless, safe and measurable.

🗺️

Location-Based Matching

Find donations and NGOs near you on an interactive map. Hyperlocal matching reduces logistics time.

🔐

Verified NGO Network

Every NGO on the platform is admin-verified ensuring your donations reach only trusted hands.

🏆

Waste Score & Leaderboard

Earn a personal Waste Score with every donation. Unlock badges and compete on city leaderboards.

📢

Donation Drives

NGOs can launch targeted drives — "Need 500 blankets by March 25" — with real-time progress tracking.

📊

Impact Dashboard

Live charts showing items donated, categories, city-wise distribution and delivery stats.

🔔

Smart Notifications

AI predicts upcoming shortages and alerts donors proactively before NGOs even raise requests.
