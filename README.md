# Extra2Essential — Turning Extra Into Essential

Extra2Essential is a digital platform that connects individuals and organizations who have surplus resources with NGOs and communities in need.
The platform aims to ensure that useful items such as food, clothes, books, and other essentials reach people who can benefit from them.

By creating a bridge between donors and receivers, Extra2Essential promotes responsible resource sharing and supports communities through technology.

---

## 🌍 Problem

Every day, many useful resources remain unused or are discarded even though they are still in good condition.
At the same time, many individuals and communities struggle to access basic necessities.

The lack of a simple and efficient system to connect donors with people who need these resources leads to missed opportunities for sharing and support.

---

## 💡 Solution

Extra2Essential provides a platform where individuals and organizations can easily donate surplus items, while NGOs and communities can request and receive them.

The platform acts as a bridge that helps redistribute resources effectively and ensures they reach those who need them.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* 📦 Donate surplus items such as food, clothes, books, and other essentials
* 🤝 Connect donors with NGOs and communities
* 📍 Location-based matching for nearby donations
* 📅 Donation scheduling and pickup coordination
* 🔐 Verified NGO accounts for safe and reliable distribution
